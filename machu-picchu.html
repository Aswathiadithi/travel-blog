Why Visit Machu Picchu?

Machu Picchu is one of the New Seven Wonders of the World and a bucket-list destination for travelers. This ancient Incan citadel sits high in the Andes Mountains and offers breathtaking views and deep historical significance.

Whether you hike the Inca Trail or take the scenic train, visiting Machu Picchu is an unforgettable experience filled with awe, mystery, and natural beauty. Learn about the Incan civilization, stone architecture, and sacred temples.

Best time to visit: April to October (dry season)

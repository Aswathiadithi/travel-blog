Top Travel Tips

1. Pack Light and Smart

Take only what you need. Use packing cubes, and roll your clothes to save space.

2. Keep Digital and Paper Copies of Documents

Always carry copies of your passport, visa, insurance, and important documents.

3. Learn Basic Local Phrases

A few local words can go a long way in connecting with people and getting help.

4. Stay Hydrated and Eat Safe

Carry a water bottle, and eat food from clean places to avoid stomach issues.

5. Always Have Offline Maps

Download maps in advance in case you don’t have internet access during travel.

6. Respect Local Culture

Be polite, dress appropriately, and learn the do’s and don’ts of the region.
